Building Permit Data: The Secret Weapon for Real Estate Investors

Every new construction project, renovation, and demolition requires a building permit. These permits are public record. Yet most real estate investors never look at them. The ones who do have a massive information advantage.

What Permit Data Tells You

  • Where development is accelerating -- A spike in permits in a neighborhood means it is about to change
  • What is being built -- Commercial vs residential, new construction vs renovation
  • Who is building -- Which contractors and developers are active in your market
  • Property values -- Renovation permits signal rising property values before Zillow catches up
  • Supply pipeline -- New housing permits predict future inventory 12-18 months out

Why Most Investors Miss This

Permit data is public but fragmented. Every county has a different system. Some are online, some require in-person visits. Checking manually across even one metro area means visiting dozens of county websites daily. Nobody has time for that.
